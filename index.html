1. Why did you decide to become a web developer?

I decided to become a web developer to complement my visual design skills.

2. What is your design process?

My process is simple. I typically do several thumbnail sketches of several different designs and choose my best three designs to show my clients. We then decide together, on a final design.

3. Who are your clients?

My clients are anybody needing super ninja skills for their websites and apps. I usually work with medium to small businesses for creating websites and apps.

4. What is your average project turnaround?

It varies depending on the project. It can take from two days for a simple project up to several weeks for a more in depth project.

5. What was your background in prior to Web Development?

I was in project management and customer service therefore; I have the business skills, and the people skills to help my clients succeed on their next project without breaking their budget!
